Vision & Objective

AURA (AI Urban Response & Analysis) is a state-of-the-art automated monitoring system designed to enhance city-level safety. By leveraging Computer Vision, AURA detects road accidents and critical traffic incidents in real-time, significantly reducing the delay between occurrence and emergency response.

Problem Statement

Traditional urban monitoring relies on manual observation of thousands of CCTV feeds. This leads to human fatigue, delayed incident detection, and increased mortality rates in high-speed traffic zones. AURA automates this surveillance, providing 24/7 vigilant oversight without loss of attention.

Dataset Details

The model is trained on a custom-curated dataset from Roboflow, incorporating:

  • Over 12,000 annotated accident scenes.
  • Vehicular impact variants (frontal, side-sweep, pileups).
  • Diverse environmental conditions (rain, fog, night-vision).
  • Shadow and occlusion handling datasets.
